About Us

At Newman Ministry International, we are committed to unveiling the unsearchable riches of Christ to all people. Through the transformative message of Jesus Christ, we help turn lives from the darkness of sin to the light of God's love. Whether you're seeking hope, truth, or a deeper relationship with God, our mission is to guide you toward spiritual transformation, faith in Jesus Christ, and a Christ-centered life. Join us as we share the message of salvation, offer biblical teaching and walk with you on your journey toward God's purpose.
