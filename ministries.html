Our Ministries

Discover how Newman Ministries International serves God and the community through different ministries.

Youth Ministry 👩‍🎓👨‍🎓

The Youth Ministry is a vibrant community of young people passionate about growing in Christ, building strong friendships, and making a difference in the world. Through worship, Bible study, mentorship, and fun activities, we provide a safe space where young people can discover their purpose and develop their God-given gifts.

Highlights

  • 🎶 Weekly Youth Fellowship: Praise, worship, and Bible discussions.
  • ❤️ Mentorship Programs: Guidance from leaders who care.
  • 🎉 Fun Activities: Camps, retreats, and outreach missions.
  • 🙌 Leadership Training: Equipping youth to serve.
Join Youth Ministry

Children’s Ministry 👧👦

Our Children’s Ministry is dedicated to nurturing young hearts and minds with the love of Christ. Through engaging Bible lessons, fun activities, and worship designed for kids, we create a safe and joyful environment where children can grow in faith and friendship.

Highlights

  • 📖 Interactive Bible Lessons: Helping children understand God’s Word.
  • 🎨 Creative Activities: Crafts, songs, and storytelling.
  • 🙏 Prayer & Worship: Teaching children to connect with God early.
  • ❤️ Loving Community: Safe and caring environment for all kids.
Join Children’s Ministry

Women’s Ministry 💐

Our Women’s Ministry is the heartbeat of hospitality, creating a warm and welcoming environment for both church members and visitors. Rooted in fellowship and God’s Word, we embrace love, care, and service as we support one another and the community.

  • 🌸 Fellowship: Growing together in faith and sisterhood.
  • ❤️ Care & Support: Encouraging and uplifting each other.
  • ❤️ Hospitality: Welcoming guests with warmth and kindness.
  • 🍲 Service: Preparing meals and refreshments for gatherings.
Join Women’s Ministry

Men’s Ministry 💪

The Men’s Fellowship is a brotherhood built on faith, strength, and service. We come together to study God’s Word, grow in leadership, and support one another in every aspect of life. Our mission is to be men of integrity, prayer, and positive impact both in church and in the community.

  • 📖 Fellowship: Studying the Word and growing spiritually.
  • 💪 Brotherhood: Encouraging one another as men of faith.
  • ❤️ Service: Leading by example in church and community.
  • 🙏 Prayer: Standing in the gap for families and the nation.
Join Men’s Ministry

Music Ministry 🎶🎤

The Music Ministry leads the congregation in worship and praise, creating an atmosphere where hearts are lifted and lives are transformed. Through songs, instruments, and harmony, we glorify God and prepare the church to receive His Word.

  • 🎤 Worship: Leading the church into God’s presence.
  • 🎹 Talent: Using music gifts to glorify the Lord.
  • 🙌 Praise: Celebrating God with joyful songs.
  • 💪 Teamwork: Serving together in unity and excellence.

Outreach Ministry 🌍

The Outreach Ministry extends the love of Christ beyond the church walls. Through evangelism, missions, and acts of compassion, we reach out to communities, sharing the Gospel and meeting physical and spiritual needs.

  • 📢 Crusades: Spreading the Gospel through open-air meetings
  • 🏠 Children’s Homes: Supporting and ministering to vulnerable kids
  • 🎓 Preaching in Institutions: Sharing the Word in schools & colleges
  • 🌱 Evangelism: Witnessing and discipling for Christ
