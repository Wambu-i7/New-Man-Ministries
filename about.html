OUR MISSION, VISSION & VALUES

Unveiling the unsearchable riches of Christ, bringing light, love, and transformation to all people.

Our Vision

At Newman Ministry International, our vision is to see a Christ-centered generation arise, rooted firmly in the Word of God and empowered by the Holy Spirit to impact families, communities, and nations. We desire to build a people who walk in truth, love, and holiness—shining as lights in a world that desperately needs hope. By nurturing spiritual growth, raising godly leaders, and equipping believers to serve with excellence, we envision a global community transformed by the power of Jesus Christ, bringing glory to God in every sphere of life.

Our Mission

Our mission at Newman Ministry International is to faithfully preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ with love and boldness, drawing people into a personal relationship with Him. We are committed to discipling believers into maturity through sound biblical teaching, prayer, and fellowship, so that they may live transformed lives rooted in Christ. Through evangelism, missions, and acts of service, we seek to reach the lost, heal the broken, and extend God’s love to all. As a community of faith, our mission is to create an environment where people experience God’s presence, grow in unity, and are equipped to impact the world for His glory.

Our Core Values

  • Faith in Christ – Jesus is our foundation
  • Love & Compassion – Serving with Christ’s heart
  • Biblical Truth – God’s Word is our authority
  • Fellowship & Unity – Growing together as one body
  • Service & Outreach – Extending God’s love to all
